Episode 3

July 20, 2017

Featuring Afrofuturist author and speculative fiction writer Ayize Jama-Everett. Ayize is the author of the Science/speculative fiction novels The Liminal People and is an amazing and thought provoking thinker. He is a world traveler that has adopted many of his experiences in to his work. Much like myself, Ayize is a committed learner, observer and listener. He's a wonderfully complex story teller and inspiring artist. Enjoy!
